Vaporwave Battler is a turn based trip back in time where you'll use every gnarly move you've got to fight through the best the 1980s and 1990s has to throw at you.

  • Turn based single player action with random enemies and moves thrown at you for a more roguelike experience -- no two runs are ever the same

  • Set in the glowy retro neon future

  • Pixel art as you remember it under a CRT haze

  • 10 unique enemies to encounter

  • 10 unique moves to unleash

  • How many bad guys can you take down?
